About Us

Welcome to the Redeem Christian Church Of God, where we're passionate about encountering God, growing in faith, and loving one another. We're thrilled that you're here!

Our Vision

Our vision is to see lives transformed by the power of the gospel and communities impacted by the love of Christ.

Our Mission

At the Redeem Christian Church Of God, our mission is to glorify God by making disciples of Jesus Christ who love God, love others, and serve the world.

History and Heritage

Established in year 2024, The Redeem Christian Church Of God has been a beacon of hope and faith in our community for four months. From humble beginnings in a small chapel to our present-day campus, we've been faithfully serving and proclaiming the good news of Jesus Christ.
